Welcome to GREAT OUTDOORS!

An e-commerce dropshipping business for now, we have future plans for opening a physical store in London Ontario. Our products focus on essential hiking equipment like backpacks, boots, and tents. Featuring all different sizes and weights, you can be sure to find the perfect set of gear to satisfy your next hiking trip whether it be casual short trail or a longer mountainous trail! Products are made from locally sourced manufacturers to support local businesses and keep prices as well as shipping cheap.

Got any questions? Make sure to reach out in our contact section as the team will be always happy to help with your needs!

About the Team

Chris - CEO

Longtime hiking coach and eager to spread the joys of hiking to all kinds of people. Unsatisfied with the quality of local hiking gear, Chris took production into his own hands to ensure customers receive only the highest grade gear they can depend on. Years of experience help Chris make sure customers know how to handle their purchases, and is always ready to lend a hand to those who need additional help.

Andrea - Product Manager

Local fitness mogul, Andrea is no stranger to managing brands with two successfully launched stores involving camping wear and gym apparel. She makes sure consumers can get what they want in the highest quality. Avid hiker herself, Andrea knows what works and what doesn't in hiking equipment.

Joey - Marketing Manager

Joey is an experienced marketing manager who has already made a notable impact on our processes for advertising and merchandising. Joey is passionate about being able to reach new types of customers because he wants people to enjoy the outdoors like he has. As a hobby, Joey organizes local hiking fundraisers and outdoor rock climbing competitions.
